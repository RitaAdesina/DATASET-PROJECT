INTRODUCTION¶

We have a dataset of above 10,000 that is dervived from the movie industry. It includes information about the popularity of various genres of movies, including the run time of the movies, and how viewers rated the movies. Additional information such as the budget of each movies,the production companies, the directors, and cast of the movies was also disclosed as this might be among the various reasons why a particular movie might turn out the way it did, in terms of average votes and popularity. The genre and release year of the movie is also an essential information to draw up any reasonable conclusion.

QUESTION FOR ANALYSIS¶

  1. How did the release year affect popularity
  2. Which genre has the highest popularity?
  3. What is the average popularity of each genre?
  4. Did the production company affect the popularity?
  5. Which Gnere produced more movies?
